# Founder-Grade Section Template

Use this structure for every major section in `outputs/report_sections/*.md`.

## Section name

### Dominant pattern
One paragraph naming the pattern in plain language.

### What people actually say
- 3 to 8 verbatim lines
- each line tagged with thread_id

### Conversation snapshots
- thread title
- what the OP is going through
- how commenters respond
- where there is disagreement or reinforcement

### What this means commercially
- why this matters for conversion
- what kind of angle it opens
- what kind of angle it kills

### Contradictions
- where the market splits
- where evidence is thin
